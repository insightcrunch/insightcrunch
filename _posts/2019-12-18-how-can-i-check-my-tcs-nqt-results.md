## Check your TCS NQT Results

You can check your TCS NQT Results in the TCS official portal within 1 week of the TCS NQT Exam Date. You will be presented with your result details after you login with your candidate registration credentials. Your score will help determine your selection in the NQT exam. The cut off and passing marks in the TCS NQT is a relative score, meaning that it varies based on the overall performance of the candidates. If many candidates perform well, the cut off mark can dramatically increase. Whereas if the questions are hard, the cut off mark will come down to accommodate more candidates.

You need to work on each and every mark in the TCS NQT exam. Due to the amount of competition and quality of candidates appearing for the NQT exam, it is essential that you give your best to every question. This means that your preparation for the TCS NQT exam has to start months before the actual exam. Many of the skills like quantitative aptitude and logical reasoning might be familiar if you have been studying for other competitive exams.
