## Overview

Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test (NQT) is the exam that many software engineer is aspiring for to be a part of the software industry today. The exam provides candidates an opportunity to enroll in one of the many job profiles offered by TCS. If you have a CodeVita background, you may even land up in one of the research and development teams in TCS. ## TCS NQT exam cost

The TCS NQT exam is free. The entire TCS recruitment process is free at every stage. So there is no payment or cost involved. However, I would have seen candidates who train and study harder often come out as the top qualifying achievers. The extensive practice tests I shared above in every topic consists of some of the best questions that you can attempt in limited time. If you feel you are confident and ready to evaluate yourself, I suggest check out these mock tests above and see how much you can score. This will also give you an idea of what to expect in the actual exam.

Some of the most decisive qualifying questions in the TCS NQT exam comes from the quantitative and logical reasoning sections. Hence even if you are strong in aptitude, it is essential to practice a lot so that you build up your speed before the exam. Saving crucial seconds in every question will help you free up essential time if you get stuck in some problem. Investing time and energy in building up your skills will pay you back rich dividends in the future. Thus prioritize creating an action plan that you can adhere to and update it frequently to reach a refined TCS NQT strategy. With a strong plan you will be able to see which topics you are skipping during the week and which are getting more attention. Accordingly you can adjust your preparation.
