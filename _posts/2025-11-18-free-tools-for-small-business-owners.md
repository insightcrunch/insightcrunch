### Generating Strong Passwords

Every business account needs a unique, strong password: your bank, your payment processor, your email, your website hosting, your social media accounts, your accounting software, your CRM, your POS system. Generate a unique password for every account, save it in your browser's built-in password manager, and eliminate the single biggest security vulnerability most small businesses have.

The financial consequences of a password breach for a small business can be devastating. Unauthorized access to your payment processor or bank account can result in direct financial loss. A compromised email account can be used to send fraudulent invoices to your clients, destroying trust that took years to build. A breached social media account can damage your brand reputation. Five seconds spent generating a unique strong password prevents all of these scenarios.

Small businesses are disproportionately targeted by credential-stuffing attacks, where hackers use email-password combinations leaked from one service to try logging into other services. These attacks are automated and operate at massive scale: millions of leaked credentials are tested against thousands of popular services within hours of a breach being made public. If your business email password is the same as your password for any service that has ever been breached, your email account is vulnerable. If your email account is compromised, the attacker can reset passwords for every other service connected to that email, cascading into a complete takeover of your business's digital presence.

The solution is straightforward and free: a unique, strong password for every account. The Strong Password Generator makes this effortless. Generate a password, copy it, save it in your browser's password manager, move on. Do this for every account your business uses. The total investment in time is perhaps thirty minutes for your initial round of password updates, and five seconds per new account going forward. The protection it provides is incalculable.
