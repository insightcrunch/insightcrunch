Have been recently viewing a program. What it going to be like with the advent of technology in the next few decades. Days of communication technology are over with nearly every sphere of our life being saturated with communicating devices of all sorts. Its going to be the visual feeling of the physical presence of our friends and families who are far off that's going to come to our homesnext. Soundsaconfusing?

![Artificial Intelligence Evolution](/assets/images/blog/blog-42.webp)
Artificial Intelligence Evolution

Its more simple than we imagine. Those who have seen the Cisco Telepresence advertisements can get an idea about it. But this is just a small part of what we are going to achieve. Artificial Intelligence,or AI,is just on the verge of going to bring on a whole new revolution in the human world. Breaking the…limits of the powers provided to us by nature. Multiplying the thinking and processing  power of human brains to enable them do something we couldn’t have ever imagined before. Creating machines more intelligent than us could also pose a more fundamental problem. If their intelligence be more than us, they would control us instead of we controlling them. Soon the age would be of a master and slave relationship. Joining  hands with our intelligent creations could be the ideal solution and a crucial step in making our world a more advanced and an incredible place to live in.
