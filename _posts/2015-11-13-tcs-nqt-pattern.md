## Overview

The Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test is one of the most comprehensive exams conducted by a software services organization today. The exam consists of questions from a broad range of topics. Being familiar with the TCS NQT pattern of questions will help you prepare well for the exam. Hundreds of thousands of candidates every year take this exam and hope to be a part of the software services industry. Succeeding in the TCS NQT exam opens up your door to a variety of profiles and careers in the technology landscape. Preparing for the TCS NQT exam starts weeks and months before the actual exam. Brushing up your key skills on the fundamental topics that you are expert in or have studied is essential. Take the mock tests and TCS practice exams below to enhance your skills and learn what kind of questions to expect and the TCS NQT pattern. These will ensure that you do not get any surprise during the actual exam.

## TCS NQT Exam Pattern

The TCS NQT exam consists of two parts, the Fundamental section and the Advanced section. The Fundamental section will evaluate your Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Verbal Ability skills in about an hour and half. The Advanced section will evaluate you on Advanced Aptitude, Advanced Logical Reasoning, and Advanced Coding skills in about an hour and half. If you have been taking the TCS practice exams above then you can be assured that you are well-prepared and know what kind of questions to expect. The advanced coding questions need you to prepare well and practice sufficiently so that you are able to solve the problems in the limited time during the exam. Even if you are comfortable with quantitative aptitude, make sure to practice enough so that you do not lose easy marks. The same applies for coding questions on arrays, matrix, and data structures. For verbal skills and coding, if you can apply the Premium questions above, you can be sure you are ready for the TCS NQT exam.
