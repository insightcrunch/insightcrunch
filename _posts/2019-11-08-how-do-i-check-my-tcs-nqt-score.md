## Overview

The Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test (NQT) is an essential test for candidates who are looking forward to be a part of TCS. Achieving a good score in the TCS NQT exam will help you choose from a variety of different TCS job profiles. The TCS NQT is a large scale evaluation test that helps TCS understand the essential capabilities of each individual. Based on the assessment results there are a variety of profiles which candidates get assigned to. The salary for each of these profiles are different, hence scoring well in the TCS NQT exam can kickstart your career with a decent salary.

The TCS NQT exam consists of quantitative aptitude questions, logical reasoning questions, verbal ability questions, and advanced coding questions. So it comprises of every possible aspect of an all-rounded evaluation exam that I can expect from a software engineer. Preparing for the NQT exam is the most crucial part, and that too strategically. Which topics would you like to improve upon? Do you know which topic you are weak in? Should you focus more on the C programs? How comfortable are you with advanced coding? ## How to check TCS NQT Score

Your TCS NQT score will be available to you in the TCS portal within 1 week of the exam. Only refer official sources and websites to confirm your TCS NQT score. Either you will be notified in your email from an official TCS email id, or the TCS website will reflect your score. TCS has streamlined their entire process of TCS NQT very efficiently so that hundreds of thousands of candidates can be processed accurately in minimal time. If you face any difficulty please reach out to the TCS contact details given above. You can also reach out to me in case you need help with a specific situation.

The TCS NQT exam is evaluated and scored based on a relative cut off model. This means if the majority of the candidates find the question paper hard, the cut off marks gets automatically lowered. Also if the paper is easy then the cut off marks gets higher. Exhaustive practice with speed and accuracy can help you become an ideal candidate for the TCS NQT exam. Once you receive your TCS NQT results, the processing for your subsequent stages will begin for recruitment if selected. TCS will notify you in their portal or via official email communications.
