## Overview

Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test (NQT) is one of the most competitive exams in the software industry today. The TCS NQT exam helps candidates be a part of TCS and get a job in one of the various profiles offered by the organization. The evaluation exam consists of a variety of topics. The TCS NQT cut-off marks vary every year and is based on the complexity of the exam and the relative performance of the candidates. The TCS NQT salary varies between based on your performance in the exam and which TCS job profile you qualify for. Hence, it is important to prepare yourself comprehensively for all the topics before appearing for the exam. The preparation for the exam starts several months before the actual exam. Be ready to brush up all your skills that you learned especially during your learning and educative years. The sheer volume of aspirants of the TCS NQT makes it one of the most coveted and aspired tests.

## TCS NQT Salary

The TCS NQT salary varies between 3 lakhs to 11 lakhs per year. For the TCS Ninja profile, the salary is about 3.5 lakhs per year. For TCS Digital profile, the salary is about 6.5 lakhs per year. For TCS Innovator profile, the salary is around 11 lakhs. Your eligibility criteria for the different profiles is largely decided by your score in the TCS NQT exam. For some top-scorers the TCS Digital profile dream comes true. If you have been a top performer in the CodeVita challenges then your TCS Innovator dream may come true. The TCS Innovator profile, as the name suggests, is focused on research and development. There is plenty of room for growth and opportunity to move to different fields once you join in the TCS Ninja or TCS Digital profile. Once you get selected via TCS NQT in any of the profiles, your salary will increase every year based on your performance, designation, and promotion. For every level there is a range of salary.
