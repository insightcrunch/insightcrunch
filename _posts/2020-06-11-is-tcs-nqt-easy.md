## Overview

The Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test (NQT) consists of various sections like the Foundation and Advanced section. The test is taken by hundreds of thousands of candidates every year who aspire to be a part of the largest software corporations in the world. Qualifying in the NQT exam opens up the door to a world of different possibilities and software engineering job profiles. The salary of a software engineer will also vary considerably based on how they perform in the NQT exam.

Brushing up all your skills that you learned all through your career is essential for the TCS NQT exam.  If you meet the TCS NQT eligibility criteria, you will be facing a lot of challenging questions and competing against some of the best candidates. I have created a fine collection of questions here with practice tests on a variety of topics. When you feel you are ready take my practice tests to get an understanding how well you prepared for the TCS NQT exam. It is of paramount importance that you pay attention to every section of the TCS NQT exam so that you do not end up giving away easy marks. I have come across candidates who were overly confident in data structures and logical reasoning that it cost them quite a few marks in the end.

## TCS NQT Difficulty

Strategic preparation for several months will help you master the TCS NQT exam syllabus. With planned study it is not difficult to crack the TCS NQT. If you are able to score well in my practice tests above you can be sure you are ready for the actual exam, else you will also know which topics you need to prepare better. The TCS NQT Foundation Section will require you to answer questions from Numerical Ability, Verbal Ability, and Reasoning Ability. The TCS NQT Advanced Section will require you to answer questions from Advanced Quantitative Aptitude, Advanced Logical Reasoning Ability, and Advanced Coding.

The difficulty of the TCS NQT exam has been highlighted to me by candidates from diverse backgrounds. In a vast majority of the cases I have seen this can be attributed to the different skill sets of each of the candidates, and their unique set of strengths and weaknesses. The TCS NQT thus continues to be portray a relative difficulty pattern among the aspirants. Whereas one person might highlight higher coding complexity, another candidate might share stories of complex logical reasoning. However I have seen with strong core fundamentals, you can succeed in the TCS NQT exam.
