## TCS NQT Package

The TCS NQT exam will help you qualify for different roles in TCS. If you have scored well int he CodeVita you can qualify for the TCS Innovator as well with a package of 11 lakhs per year. The most common profiles via the TCS NQT are the TCS Ninja with an average package of 3.5 lakhs per year, and the TCS Digital with an average package of 6.5 lakhs per year. It is important to remember that your monthly salary is usually not the equivalent of dividing your yearly package by 12, factor in tax and provident fund deductions. Also, within a few years you can move to different roles via learning new skills and based on your performance. There are well-defined criteria for promotion every few years up to a certain level and accordingly your package in TCS will incrementally move to higher brackets. Each of the profiles caters to different technical and research oriented domains within TCS. Hence, if you have a knack or inclination to a specific technology or area of work, make sure to keep learning. And when the opportunities present themselves you will be in a good spot to move to your new role.
