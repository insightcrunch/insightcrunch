## Overview

The Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier (NQT) Test is conducted every year by TCS. The TCS NQT is one of the most widely anticipated exams by aspirants and takes months of preparation and hard work to qualify. TCS has a high level of benchmark and cutoff that varies every year, and successful candidates get tagged to various TCS profiles when selected. Due to the huge number of candidates aspiring to succeed in the exam, the pattern of the exam is dynamic in nature with a variety of questions. You will have to answer questions that assess your numerical ability, coding ability, logical reasoning ability, verbal ability, and even questions based on your personality.

This helps TCS understand you as a complete individual before it can proceed to the next stages of hiring. I receive various questions from candidates asking how to prepare, so here you will get a complete set of mock tests that will give you a simulation of the actual exam. Only quality learning and exhaustive practice of every topic in the TCS NQT syllabus will you get a good score. Make sure to prepare well on every topic that you have ready during your career, especially programming topics and logical reasoning problems. The more you practice the better your chances are at succeeding in the TCS NQT exam.

## TCS NQT 2022 Results Date

The TCS NQT Results are going to be declared within a week of completion of the exam. If you are a candidate for TCS NQT and following all the steps in the TCS portal closely, you will find them updating regularly. Meanwhile if you succeed in getting a good score in the TCS NQT exam and selected for next steps of recruitment, you will get an email from the TCS Recruitment Team on the next steps of the process. If you have queries you can email at the official TCS email at careers@tcs.com and wait for their response. Moreover you can also comment below or email me if you do not want to wait or have a specific situation and want to understand how to proceed.

Given the current job market and demand pipeline, you can often expect a delay in the response and project allocation of new employees. There is a direct relation between the region where you are appearing for the exam, the demand of your skillsets in the projects in your vicinity, and the number of candidates who have that skillset. Based on these factors you will be assigned to a project after your TCS NQT results are declared. Significantly your TCS NQT results will also determine which profile you will get tagged to like Ninja and Digital, and that will also decide your yearly salary. Hence every mark matters in your exam, get a feel of the what the difficulty of the questions are like from my practice tests shared above. If you can score well in my practice tests, you can be assured that you are moving in the right direction with your study plan.
