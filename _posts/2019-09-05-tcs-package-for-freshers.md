## Overview

Tata Consultancy Services provides attractive package to thousands of software engineers every year. TCS conducts a variety of recruitment drives to assess candidates in minimum time. The official TCS portals help candidates register with their details if they meet the eligibility criteria. After the different rounds of exams and interviews, candidates are selected by the software company to undergo internal TCS trainings. Successful completion of the training makes candidates eligible for project. At this project readiness stage candidates are allocated based on their skill sets and project requirements.

The salary for professionals working in TCS varies over the years. There are promotions and increments that happen in the system automatically every few years upto a certain level. Beyond a certain level your promotion is based on your performance. Salary increments are defined by your performance and thus can vary among professionals with the same experience. TCS provides a number of platforms and tools to improve your learning even while you are allocated to a specific project. The more you invest in your learning the better rewards you can expect in the future.

## TCS Package for Freshers

The TCS Package for Freshers is 3.5 lakhs INR. I often get questions from candidates regarding the monthly salary, is it 3.5 lakhs divided by 12? The answer is no. Due to tax and other deductions you can expect your monthly salary to be 3.5 lakhs divided by 14 or 15. The number that you will get will represent your monthly salary. Your initial salary during the TCS training period may be lower than what you would expect. Successful completion fo the training will reinstate your salary to what you were expecting. You will receive an  annual compensation letter from TCS as well every year.

One of the most common questions I came across from candidates is how to prepare for the TCS Project Readiness Assessment (PRA) exams. In these exams you would be required to show required skills that are essential in the software industry today. Be prepared to be tested on questions that measure your ability to write codes as well. How well can you handle algorithms in the most popular programming languages? Can you answer my practice questions below? You can be sure you are on the right track if you can comfortably answer my questions here. Else review each of the topics again before you arrive for the actual TCS assessment exams.
