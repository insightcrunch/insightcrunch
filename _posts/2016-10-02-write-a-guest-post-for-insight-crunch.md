Insight Crunch is the most happening place for discussions and information on the IT sector, the latest technology, and the gadgets that emerge. This site is viewed by thousands every day and have dedicated readers. **You are welcome to share your experience, and write articles and contribute to this site.** Your originality and creativity will be appreciated, and your views will be heard by thousands and stimulate healthy discussions.

## Payments

Articles if accepted can help you earn between $10 to $50 based on the topic. All decisions by the editorial team are final. Feedback to improve a submission may be provided if editors are interested in the article. If your article is not approved, you can submit again with revisions. Payment methods will be discussed and confirmed over email.

## Quality Requirements

I am looking for creative articles on various IT work cultures, exam experiences, interviews, hiring journeys, and stories that is beneficial to your peers. 

- Once you are ready with your article, verify your content in [**Grammarly**](https://www.grammarly.com/). Ideally, I would expect no red highlights by the quality checker. 

- Verify if your content is at least **1500 words** long. Make **short paragraphs** of no more than 5 sentences, and sometimes even an occasional one-sentence paragraph is fine. If you feel or if you are in doubt if you should edit a portion for enhanced clarity, in most cases you should. The reader will always find it harder to read your content than you. 

- **Use heading or subheadings** after every 10-15 sentences. No one wants to read a long block of text or several small blocks of text with no intuitive feel of context. Headings should clearly depict what the following content is about.

- Verify your content in [**Plagiarism Detector**](https://plagiarismdetector.net/). The ideal Unique score is at least 95%. Plagiarism red flags due to accidental content matches with unrelated sites, or when quoting a few words from a leading site, is okay. Impress your readers with your originality.

Once you are ready with your article, upload your story above for me to review. You can be completely anonymous, or use a pseudo name. Or, you can also add links to your social media profiles if you want so that people can learn more about you.
