## Overview

The Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test (NQT) is one of the most popular tests today. The assessment test is taken by candidates from various locations, and it becomes particularly important to focus on the flexibility. It is important to learn about the TCS NQT in-center vs remote pros and cons before you proceed. The exam comprises different topics of varied difficulty that you will need to answer. The time provided for the exam is just about sufficient if you are well aware of the concepts. So it is important to practice and strengthen your fundamentals.

The TCS iON is a strategic unit of Tata Consultancy Services that is focused on learning and educational engagements. The demand for online and proctored exams and practice tests has grown over the years. Not only does online exams and the required infrastructure provide the reach and opportunity to evaluate more candidates in lesser time, it also helps level the playing field in uncertain situations due to weather and commute challenges. The technological advancements and the ability of large corporations to leverage the benefits of such educational platforms is indeed valuable today.

## TCS NQT In-center

The TCS NQT In-center Assessment System is my recommended way to take the exam. Even though the robust proctoring and assessment mechanism of Remote approach takes care of answers evaluation, the in-center approach has its own advantages. You do not have to worry about connectivity issues and the setup where you are taking the test. Your exam center will provide you all the facilities and environment needed to take the exam. In case of any challenges you need not worry as there will be people to assist you. The subjective answers are assessed by an evaluator, similar to what happens in the TCS NQT Remote exam. Scores are digitally processed and published, reducing the time taken to announce your results. Many reputed institutions have started using the reliable TCS iON Remote Assessments to conduct their exams. TCS iON provides an efficient IT-as-a-Service model with a secure easy to use experience.

## TCS NQT Remote

The TCS iON Remote Assessment System provides products that enable universities and exam boards to conduct secure, free, and fair examinations at scale, and in a remote model. During the pandemic these mechanisms proved especially useful. Candidates can appear in the TCS NQT exam remotely if they opt in for TCS NQT Remote. The TCS iON Remote Assessments enable control at different levels based on the institution's requirements. Your device (desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone) will be locked down and only will be allowed to capture exam responses. A video stream will also be sent to a central server in the cloud that will be used to detect malpractice attempts using artificial intelligence and machine learning frameworks. TCS NQT Remote invigilators can be alerted immediately. Invigilators also have the ability to proctor candidates in real-time from anywhere.
