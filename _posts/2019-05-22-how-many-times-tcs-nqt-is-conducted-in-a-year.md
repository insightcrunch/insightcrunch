## Overview

Tata Consultancy Services National Qualifier Test (NQT) is one of the top assessment tests taken by aspiring candidates today. One of the most common questions I have come across candidates asking me is how many times the TCS NQT is conducted in a year. The unpredictability and predictability of the various aspects of the TCS NQT exam is what makes it so special. On one hand we know how to prepare thoroughly and what questions to focus on in the TCS NQT exam. Accordingly we prepare our practice questions and mock tests. On the other hand we have a variety of factors that we need to understand to learn how many times the TCS NQT exam will be conducted this year.

TCS is a large organization with nearly a million employees. The TCS NQT exam is aimed at evaluating a large amount of candidates in minimal time in an efficient way. The exam consists of a variety of questions and tests, both topic wise and complexity wise that help evaluate a candidate holistically. You will be tested upon all the skills and qualities that you need to have in the software services industry. Do you know a variety of technologies? Are you proficient in more than one programming language? I would like to know you better. So make sure to practice harder, especially the advanced coding problems.

## Yearly TCS NQT Quota

The TCS NQT exam is conducted every year variable nuumber of times. For example in the last few years we have seen the TCS NQT exam being conducted quarterly, and we have also seen the TCS NQT exam being conducted once in a year. Hence based on the demand of the market and the project pipelines that TCS has in the specific year, it is decided how many new candidates are required. Accordingly the TCS NQT exam is held. The TCS attrition rate also is a determining factor in understanding the necessity of conducting the TCS NQT exam. New candidates may be required to fill in if there is large scale exodus.

The best thing that you can do while anticipating for the next TCS NQT exam announcement is prepare yourself. Practice my advanced coding, aptitude, logical reasoning, and verbal skills questions and mock tests given here. Make sure to be able to solve every question within the time limit. For any mistakes learn from the feedbacks given below the questions to make sure you do not repeat it in the main TCS NQT exam. What sets apart candidates amongst the thousands during this exam is how accurately you can answer the questions where every marks count in the limited time.
