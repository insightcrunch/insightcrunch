It has been an amazing journey. Right after school when I stepped in to my college, I had not the least idea what it would be like. It was just a queer feeling. Finally, it all happened, studied, made a lot of cool friends, prepared and sat for campussing and got a job...and today, on our farewell day, it was just simply so much heart-breaking...so many days we had spent together, it was extremely hard to part our ways.. Maybe we would meet later, have parties, arrange trips on vacations, meet in our professional lives, but the days spent together in classes, the addas in the tea-shops are all priceless memories are never to be forgetten .. I made a small video for all my friends which was shown on our farewell. Some moments of the millions we cherished together... (don't forget to turn on your speakers :)
