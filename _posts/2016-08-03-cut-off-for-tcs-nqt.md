## Cut off Marks for TCS NQT

The cut off marks for the TCS NQT exam is determined by how the overall population is performing. Now, we know the main sections in the TCS NQT exam are Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Advanced Coding, and Verbal Skills. If the complexity of the questions are high in one of the sections, it will bring the cut off marks down. This is because the majority of the candidates will be scoring less in that section. The same goes for the other sections, the harder the questions, the lower the cut off marks. But this does not mean more candidates get qualified, because the majority will be scoring less as well. Likewise, if the questions are on the easier side, the marks are easily scored. The cut off thus dramatically increases, and you will need to be at your best to answer every question accurately and within time. Speed and accuracy is the key to achieving the cut off marks in the TCS NQT exam. You may be a great problem solver, but if you take more time then you will need to work hard in improving your speed.
