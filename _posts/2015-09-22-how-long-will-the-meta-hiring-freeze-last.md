## Meta Hiring Freeze Duration

The hiring target of Meta has been reduced by about a third in the current year from its initial plan. Meta is also leaving many of the Meta job positions unfilled in response to the ongoing attrition. Meta has turned up the heat on performance management to weed out staffers who are not able to meet aggressive delivery targets. This is resulting in poor work-life balance for Meta employees as well. The Meta management has observed there are employees in Meta who should not be there. And setting more aggressive goals is the path Meta has taken to let employees decide for themselves if they want to deliver or quit their job. The profitability of Meta has taken a hit with macroeconomic pressures and increasing data privacy hits to the Facebook Ads business. The Meta hiring process is expected to continue slowly at least till the first half of the next financial year. Meta focuses to build on the systems and infrastructure it already has and the challenges it is already facing rather than invest and hire more on newer systems and opportunities.

## Is Meta in trouble?

Meta is in deep waters and facing challenges in multiple fronts, mainly on data privacy and profitability from its advertisement business. Meta has seen slowing ad sales and also slow user growth with rivals like TikTok and Snapchat showing higher user adoption with exciting new features and algorithms. The United States economy overall is also showing signs of a slower growth. Meta has been hit more severely than its competitors Apple and Google. Meta has seen record decline in its active users which means that existing users are using Meta products like Facebook, Instagram, and Whatsapp much lesser than before. Rivals products like TikTok, Snapchat, Flickr, Telegram, and Signal are getting adopted by a larger population than ever before. The algorithm of Meta is currently being revised to focus around the discovery model that TikTok has received huge success with. The other primary segment Meta is focused on is an expense Metaverse virtual reality technology that will take its time to mature. Meta's troubles might be a short term one if it can recover its financial resources with Instagram Reels, headset Cambria, and similar other aspects of its revenue model.
