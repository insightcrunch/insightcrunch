## Overview

Tata Consultancy Services is one of the largest software services organizations in the world. With nearly a million employees, it is home to one of the largest labor force in the world today. A vast workforce as large as TCS needs efficient planning and a flexible management. TCS has a variety of roles in its management positions for which it conducts the TCS MBA hiring drive to fill in with skilled professionals. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria will be able to participate in this drive. To find if you are eligible please use the TCS MBA Eligibility Checker Tool below.

The number of candidates appearing for this assessment tests every year is huge. There is always a demand for efficient management candidates in TCS due to the large number of projects worked upon. Your quantitative aptitude skills, logical reasoning skills, and verbal skills will be put to the ultimate test where every mark will count. The 90 minutes TCS selection test will have 47 questions where 20 questions are from quantitative aptitude, 20 questions from business aptitude, and 7 questions from verbal aptitude. If you can answer my questions below from each of these topics, you can be sure you are on the right track. Otherwise, make a note of each real-time feedback and revise these topics. Keep in mind that you need to be comfortable in my exams to score well in the actual exam.

The TCS MBA eligibility criteria checker tool above comprehensively evaluates your profile and will tell you if you are eligible to appear for the TCS MBA hiring drive. You must have completed 2 years of full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Management Studies (MMS), Post Graduate Diploma in Business Analytics (PGDBA), Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) in Marketing, Finance, Operations Supply Chain Management, Information Technology, General Management, Business Analytics, or Project Management to apply. You also need to be from a technology background before pursuing MBA. TCS mentions that you cannot have academic gap more than 2 years, and any gap must have supporting documents. At most 2 backlogs are allowed at the time of appearing for the drive, and all of your backlogs should be cleared at the time of joining TCS if you are selected at the end of the TCS MBA recruitment drive.

To succeed in the evaluation exam for TCS MBA, you need to have strong aptitude and analytical skills. Speed and accuracy will be key to succeed in this time-limited exam. Strategically plan your study schedule and practice a variety of problems on quantitative aptitude, advanced logical reasoning, and verbal ability skills. At the end of every practice test I have shared here, refresh to begin a new TCS MBA mock exam with new set of questions. Any time you need help I can guide you from my vast personal experience. To apply for the TCS MBA hiring drive, log in to TCS Next Step, register for 'IT' and apply for TCS MBA Hiring, click on ‘Apply For Drive’, select your mode of test and click on ‘Apply’. You can check your status using ‘Track Your Application’, and your status should reflect as ‘Applied for Drive’. TCS hires MBA students for a variety of roles like Product Specialist, Pre Sales Consulting, Business Analyst, Functional Consultant, Marketing Analyst, and Solution Architect.
