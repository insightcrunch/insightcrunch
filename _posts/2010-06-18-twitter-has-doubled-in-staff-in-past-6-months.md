Twitter, the favorite microblogging service of many including [me](http://twitter.com/Rahul_B), has nearly doubled its employee strength in the past few months., Back in 2009 they had only 22 staffs, and in early 2010 they had around 105. Now officially they have around 205 employees. This number  is nothing compared to Google or Microsoft, but this number is going to increase soon enough, with a work environment that idolizes Google.
