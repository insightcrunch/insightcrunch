<h1>About Insight Crunch</h1>

Insight Crunch is an independent blog covering technology, society, and the texture of everyday life. Written from Chicago.

The writing here doesn't follow a formula. Some posts are analytical, some are reflective, some are just observations that felt worth pinning down.

If something resonates—or doesn't—feel free to reach out.

— Rahul Bhattacharya
